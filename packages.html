Common Questions

You Might Be Wondering…

Can we legally get married in Ireland if we're not Irish?

Absolutely. Ireland welcomes couples from anywhere in the world to legally marry here. You'll need to give at least 3 months' notice to the HSE — we guide you through every step of this process.

What if the weather is bad?

This is Ireland, so we plan for this! We always have backup plans — alternative sheltered locations, scheduling flexibility, and honestly, a bit of mist or dramatic clouds often makes for the most stunning photos.

Can we bring a small number of guests?

Of course. Most of our elopements are just the couple, but we can accommodate close family or friends (typically up to 10 for most packages). The Bespoke package supports up to 20 guests.

How far in advance do we need to book?

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months ahead, especially for summer dates. However, we do our best to accommodate shorter timelines — just get in touch and we'll see what's possible.

What locations do you work in?

All across Ireland. Our most requested locations include the Cliffs of Moher, the Ring of Kerry, the Wicklow Mountains, the Aran Islands, Connemara, and the Causeway Coast. We're always open to exploring somewhere new.
