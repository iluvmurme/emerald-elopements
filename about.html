About Emerald Elopements

Meet Rachel

The Person Behind
Every Love Story

Hi, I'm Rachel — and if you're here, I'm guessing you're the kind of couple who'd rather stand on a cliff edge with the Atlantic wind in your hair than walk down a hotel aisle. I love that about you.

I founded Emerald Elopements after years in the wedding and events industry, where I kept watching couples spend enormous amounts of money and energy on a day that felt more like a performance than a celebration. I wanted to create something different — something smaller, wilder, and infinitely more meaningful.

Based in Ireland and endlessly in love with its landscapes, I handle every single detail — permits, officiant, florals, photography coordination, and everything in between — so that on your day, you don't have to think about anything except each other.

Whether you're local or flying in from the other side of the world, I'll make the whole thing feel effortless, personal, and entirely, beautifully yours.
